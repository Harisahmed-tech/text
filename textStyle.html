Photography - My Favorite Hobby

Photography has been my passion for the past three years. I love capturing moments that tell a story, whether it's a sunset over the city or candid shots of friends laughing together. There's something magical about freezing time in a single frame. I usually go out early in the morning to catch the golden hour light, which makes everything look amazing.
